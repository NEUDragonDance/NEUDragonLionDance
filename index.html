About Us

We are a group of driven college students who seek to spread Chinese culture through artful and unique performances of Chinese Dragon Dance and Lion Dance.

The Northeastern University Dragon and Lion Dance Troupe was founded in 2015 with the goal is to spread Chinese culture through Dragon Dance performances, as well as give those interested the opportunity to learn, practice, and be apart of these performances whether that be on the dragon, the pearl, or with the instrumentalists. We also began practicing and performing Lion Dance in the Spring of 2021.

Since then, we have grown to perform all over the Boston area, such as at Northeastern University’s many culture shows, at neighboring colleges and universities, and even performing the opener for the 2022 Intercollegiate Lion Dance Conference in Amherst, MA.

Special thanks to Teriyaki House for sponsoring us! Check out their restaurant at 32 W Broadway, Boston MA.
