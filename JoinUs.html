Our Team

We are a group of driven college students who seek to spread Chinese culture through artful and unique performances of Chinese Dragon Dance and Lion Dance.

We value teamwork, giving back to the community, and fostering these values through the inclusion of diversity, giving those without prior experience the opportunity to learn and get involved, as well as performing for many charitable events! We are a non-profit student performance group and are proud to perform for notable causes and events!

Join Us





Interested in joining the Northeastern University Dragon and Lion Dance Troupe? All Northeastern Students are welcome, no experience necessary!

Students are welcome to join any combination of the following:

• Dragon Dance
• Lion Dance
• Instruments

Please contact us if you have any questions!
_________

Practices during the summer are scheduled based on performance requests and member availability.

Weekly practices will resume as usual during the fall. Typically, dragon dance practices are held on Sunday nights at Cabot and lion dance practices are held on Saturday nights at East Village.

Please fill out the 2023-24 interest form to receive email updates on future practices and interest meetings!
