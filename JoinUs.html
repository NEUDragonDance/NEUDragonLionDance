Our Team

We are a Boston-based non-profit student performance group spreading Chinese culture through artful performances of traditional Chinese Dragon Dance and Lion Dance.

We value teamwork and giving unique learning opportunities for Chinese traditions. We also strive to give back to the community through our performances for notable causes and events!

Join Us





Interested in joining? All Northeastern Students are welcome!
Members may participate in any combination of Dragon Dance, Lion Dance, and Instruments (e.g. drum, gong, cymbals).

• No experience necessary (we teach starting at basics)
• Weekly practices for Dragon Dance and Lion Dance
• Cool performance opportunities!
• Very fun people and social outings :)

_________


Summer practices are scheduled based on performance requests and member availability. Weekly practices will resume as usual in the fall.

Typically, Dragon Dance practices are held on Sunday nights and Lion Dance practices on Saturday nights on campus.

Fill out the interest form for email updates on future practices and interest meetings and let us know if you have any questions!
