We are a group of driven college students who seek to spread Chinese culture through artful and unique performances of Chinese Dragon Dance and Lion Dance.

Since our founding in 2015, we have grown to perform all over the Boston area, such as at Northeastern University’s many culture shows, at neighboring colleges and universities, and even performing the opener for the 2022 Intercollegiate Lion Dance Conference in Amherst, MA.
